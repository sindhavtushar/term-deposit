About Dataset

Bank Marketing Dataset Information

The dataset is related to direct marketing campaigns (phone calls) of a Portuguese banking institution. The classification goal is to predict whether the client will subscribe to a term deposit.

Dataset Details:

  • Number of Instances: 45,211
  • Number of Attributes: 17 (including the target variable)
  • Target Variable: y (Binary: 'yes' or 'no')

Feature Description:

Feature Description
age Age of the client
job Type of job (e.g., admin, blue-collar, entrepreneur, etc.)
marital Marital status (e.g., married, single, divorced)
education Level of education
default Has credit in default? (yes/no)
balance Average yearly balance (in euros)
housing Has a housing loan? (yes/no)
loan Has a personal loan? (yes/no)
contact Contact communication type (e.g., cellular, telephone)
day Last contact day of the month
month Last contact month of year
duration Last contact duration (in seconds)
campaign Number of contacts performed during this campaign
pdays Days passed since last contact (-1 means never contacted)
previous Number of contacts before this campaign
poutcome Outcome of previous marketing campaign
y Target Variable: Has the client subscribed? (yes/no)
